Lost Wax Metal Casting



Lost Wax Metal Casting - The Universal Choice for Precision Steel Parts Lost wax metal casting is the go-to choice for manufacturers who demand precision steel parts. This age-old technique involves creating a wax model of the desired product and then coating it with several layers of ceramic. The ceramic mold is then heated to melt the wax and create an exact replica in molten metal. Once the metal cools and solidifies, the ceramic mold is broken away, leaving behind the desired steel part.
